🧪 How to Test

  1. Login as a doctor
  2. Navigate to "Manage Availability"
  3. Scroll to "Specific Dates - Leave Management" section
  4. Use calendar navigation (Previous/Next buttons)
  5. Click multiple future dates to select them
  6. Enter a reason for unavailability
  7. Click "Mark as Unavailable"
  8. Verify dates appear in "Currently Unavailable Dates" section
  9. Test removing unavailable dates using the × button
  10. Save availability and check integration with booking system
