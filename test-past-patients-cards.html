🔧 Technical Implementation

Enhanced Data Structure:

interface EnhancedPatient {
  patientId: string;
  patientName: string;
  patientEmail: string;
  cpr: string; // Civil Personal Record
  age: number;
  gender: 'male' | 'female';
  bloodType: string;
  allergies: string[];
  medicalProblems: MedicalProblem[];
  totalVisits: number;
  lastVisit: string;
}

Key Features:

🏥 Appointment-Focused
Each card represents a completed appointment with specific visit details
👤 Enhanced Patient Info
CPR, age, gender, blood type, and medical conditions for each patient
💊 Prescription Management
Write/edit prescriptions directly from appointment cards
📊 Visit Statistics
Patient stats including total visits, allergies, and visit history
🎨 Professional Design
Card-based layout with hover effects and color-coded information

🎯 Comparison: Past Patients vs Patient Records

👥 Past Patients

  • Appointment-specific view
  • Completed visits only
  • Prescription management per visit
  • Visit notes and details
  • Revenue tracking
  • Monthly grouping

📋 Patient Records

  • Comprehensive medical history
  • All appointments (past & future)
  • Complete prescription history
  • Medical conditions tracking
  • Patient demographics
  • Ongoing care management

🧪 How to Test

  1. Login as a doctor
  2. Navigate to "Past Patients" from the header menu
  3. View the enhanced patient cards with:
    • CPR numbers and demographic information
    • Medical conditions with icons and severity indicators
    • Appointment details in organized sections
    • Patient statistics and visit history
    • Prescription management buttons
  4. Use search and month filters to find specific patients
  5. Hover over cards to see interactive effects
  6. Click "Write/Edit Prescription" buttons to test functionality

📊 Benefits

  • Better Organization: Clear separation of appointment-specific data

  • Professional Appearance: Modern card-based design
  • Quick Access: Key patient information at a glance
  • Better UX: Interactive hover effects and clear visual hierarchy
